FAQ

To ensure high security, we only take orders via phone call. Please use the contact form to the right to schedule a call.
Can I cancel or modify an order?
All sales are final to ensure transperency with our sellers and buyers.

Yes, we do! We are thrilled to spread the joy of Barbies everywhere. Please let us know your request and we will see what we can do (extra charge).
What are your shipping options and rates?
We offer standard, expedited, and international shipping. Rates vary based on the destination and selected shipping method.
How long does shipping take?
Delivery times depend on the shipping method chosen. Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days within the US.
What is your return policy?
All sales are final.

Please contact us immediately. We will arrange for a replacement or refund and cover the return shipping costs.

Contact us with details and photos of your dolls. We will review and provide a purchase offer or consignment option.

Currently, we do not. However, we are looking to expand in this area in the future!
